About Chef Amaka

With over 12 years of experience in the culinary arts, I bring passion, authenticity, and a touch of modern flair to traditional Nigerian cuisine. My journey began in my grandmother's kitchen in Calabar, where I learned the secrets of creating rich, flavorful soups that tell a story.

I specialize in catering for events of all sizes, from intimate family gatherings to large weddings and corporate functions. My goal is to provide an unforgettable culinary experience that leaves your guests satisfied and delighted. Let's create something delicious together!

Services Offered

Full-Service Event Catering

Complete catering solution for weddings, birthdays, and corporate events. Includes menu planning, food preparation, and on-site service.

Private Chef Services

Enjoy a bespoke restaurant-quality dining experience in the comfort of your own home. Perfect for special occasions and dinner parties.

Bulk Food Orders (Soup Bowls)

Order my signature soups in bulk. Perfect for weekly meal prep or stocking your freezer with authentic, home-cooked meals.
